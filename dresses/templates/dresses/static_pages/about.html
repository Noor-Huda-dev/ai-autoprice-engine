About TarigWorldWide

Our story and values

Our Story

Founded in Lahore, TarigWorldWide began as a small boutique and has grown into a premier fashion destination. We specialize in high-quality unstitched fabrics and party wear collections.

Our journey started at Bhobtian Chowk and we've since expanded our retail presence across Pakistan while maintaining our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our top priority in every interaction.

Authenticity

Innovation

Continuously evolving our designs to set new fashion trends.
