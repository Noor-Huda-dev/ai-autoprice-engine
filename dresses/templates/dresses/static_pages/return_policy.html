Exchange & Return Policy

Last Updated: 31 May 2025

Our Return Policy

We offer a 14-day return policy from the date of purchase. To be eligible for a return:

  • Item must be unused and in original condition
  • Original tags must be attached
  • Must include original packaging
Note: Final sale items and intimate apparel cannot be returned.

Exchange Process

  1. Contact customer service within 14 days of delivery
  2. Provide order number and photos of item
  3. We'll email you a return authorization
  4. Ship item back to us within 7 days
  5. We'll process your exchange once received
